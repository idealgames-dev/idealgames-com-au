Current Projects

Nagi - Crappy Dragon

ANDROID · iOS · WEB
Nagi is a quirkly little endless runner for mobile and web. Avoid environmental baddies, collect coins and of coarse .. beat your friends. We focussed, perhaps too heavily, on stats in Nagi so expect some obscure challenges. Nagi is free however does contain ads · hey, we gotta eat too · if you wish, you can pay a single fee to remove all ads, forever ... but you don't have too. You will receive all future updates and expansion.


You may notice some other characters pop up in Nagi. Initially, you will see 'Chunk' from 'Funner Runner'. Dan was a huge inspiration for building Nagi and continues to impress with both in his live stream and his charming whimsey
